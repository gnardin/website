Interests

My research interests center around the use and development of Modeling and Simulation methods and tools in combination with Artificial Intelligence and Statistical Analysis for understanding the impacts of social and human behaviors on the emergent properties of complex adaptive systems.

  • Agent-Based Modeling
  • Multiagent Systems
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Simulation Data Analytics
  • Computational Social Sciences
